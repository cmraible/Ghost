TODAY’S QUOTE:

"You try buying insurance off of Obamacare," Rep. Alexandria Ocasio-Cortez told Vanity Fair in October, noting that she was paying $200-per-month as a bartender for a health insurance plan that came with the "privilege" of an $8,000 deductible.

President Joe Biden’s COVID relief proposal asks Congress to pay billions to insurers to put millions more Americans on plans like these, which many won’t be able to use — instead of creating a promised public health insurance option or expanding existing government health care programs.

  • PELOSI FAMILY BETS BIG ON TESLA: House Speaker Nancy Pelosi’s husband purchased at least $1.2 million worth of stock options last month in Tesla, Apple, and Disney.
