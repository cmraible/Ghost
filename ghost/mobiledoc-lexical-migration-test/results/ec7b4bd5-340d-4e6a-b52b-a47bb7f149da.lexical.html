Snippets are the extracts of your site that appear in search - and when your site is shared into social services like Facebook and LinkedIn. These are largely determined by the search engines themselves, but you can make suggestions which they often make use of.

I have provided examples, and links to documentation. This will need implementation by your development team, but key areas like description and photo choice should be customisable by the author or editor.

Google ran a series of useful guides in early 2019, including an FAQ and details on the new “How To” markup.
