On Charles Martinet’s Instagram page, the voice of Mario announced that he has been inducted into the Guinness World Records for Most Game Voice Over Performances as a Single Character. This feat was achieved when Martinet voiced Mario in Super Smash Bros. Ultimate, marking the performances as the 100th time.
