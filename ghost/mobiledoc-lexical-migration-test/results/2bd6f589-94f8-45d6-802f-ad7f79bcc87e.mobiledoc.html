This Tuesday January 12th at 7pm ET, Democratic Del. Lee Carter will talk to Daily Poster subscribers about his 2021 run for governor of Virginia. We will also talk to Saikat Chakrabarti, a former top aide to Rep. Alexandria Ocasio-Cortez, about his new organization pressuring Democrats to deliver on their campaign promises.

Carter is a Marine Corps veteran and self-described socialist who in 2017 defeated a top Republican leader to win a seat in the Virginia House of Delegates. He has filed paperwork to run for governor.

Chakrabarti is a former chief of staff for Rep. Alexandria Ocasio-Cortez who is now the president of New Consensus.  He recently launched a new PAC called "No Excuses" designed to hold Democrats accountable to their campaign promises. The PAC is running ads against Joe Manchin in West Virginia.
