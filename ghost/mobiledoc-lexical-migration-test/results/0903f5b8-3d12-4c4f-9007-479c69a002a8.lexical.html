Nintendo has stated that they would like to see 20-30 games released on the Switch EVERY WEEK. Fans are concerned as these games will be mostly filled with shovelware, but deep within that shovelware there will be some hidden gems on Nintendo Switch.

This list is not to list the best indie games on the system, and if that’s what you were hoping for then you can click the link right here. No, this list is to highlight those games that you may just scroll past as you’re searching for that one big title; titles that we believe deserve their chance in the spotlight.

Disclaimer: We will only list hidden gems on Nintendo Switch that we have personally played ourselves, so please feel free to list any suggestions in the Comments section below and we’ll be sure to consider it when we update this list. Also, all prices listed are in USD.

The Bug Butcher

Price $7.99
Published By 2Awesome Studio
Genre Action / Arcade
Rating E10+
Players 1 – 2

The Bug Butcher is a beautifully crafted arcade shooter that requires you to take down the bugs across 30 stages, picking up power-ups along the way to increase your chances. Whether you play in solo or co-op mode, this game is sure to bring a challenge to any skilled veteran, whilst never seeming impossible. The Bug Butcher has colorful graphics and fantastic humor to really bring so much personality in this simple but engaging arcade-inspired gem.

Damsel

Price $16.99
Developed By Screwtape Studios
Published By / Arcade / Platformer
Rating E10+
Players 1

Damsel is an arcade action platformer full of bite-sized levels for you to quickly progress through from one to the next. The plot follows Damsel, a light-footed special agent with just the right amount of sass, as she takes on the underbelly of the corporate vampire world. Uncover their sinister plots, complete your missions and put a stop to their human kidnappings.

Flat Heroes

Price $9.99
Developed By Parallel Circles
Published By Deck 13
Genre Action / Arcade / Party / Platformer
Rating E
Players 1-4

Flat Heroes is a platformer with a minimalistic art style, but the game packs in so much more than what you’d initially think. Packed with more than 300 bite-sized levels, as well as daily challenges, unlimited survival levels and online leaderboards, this game is packed with content for such a low price. The gameplay is smooth and refined, requiring you to avoid oncoming hazards from multiple directions and survive until they stop. It starts off easy, but you’ll soon find yourself dying multiple times in a single levels. Flat Heroes is certainly a game to challenge any seasoned platforming fan.

Forma.8


Price $9.99
Developed By MixedBag Srl
Published By MixedBag Srl
Genre Action / Adventure / Arcade
Rating E10+
Players 1

Being the only Metroidvania on the list, Forma.8 is a unique take on the genre. Play as Forma.8, the little exploration probe tasked with the mission to recover a powerful energy source on an alien planet. With a colorful art style and a clean presentation, Forma.8 is a rich adventure that thrives in its simplistic graphics and surprisingly deep gameplay. Forma.8 is often on sale so it’s definitely a fantastic game to consider for a steal.

Freedom Planet

Price $14.99
Developed By GalaxyTrail
Published By Marvelous (XSEED)
Genre Action / Adventure / Platformer
Rating E10+
Players 1

On its initial release in 2014, 2D Sonic fans rejoiced as Freedom Planet represented a lot of what fans of the blue hedgehog were missing. However now that we’re living in a post-Sonic Mania world, that doesn’t take away everything that is unique about this hidden gem. Play as the dragon Lilac, the feline Carol and Milla the shy puppy on a quest to restore power to the Kingdom Stone that emits energy to the three kingdoms, but is beginning to weaken. The combat takes a more action/platformer approach, with a variety of attacks to dish out to your enemies. The story is deeper than you’d expect, with every single line of dialogue delivered with quality voice acting. Definitely not one to skip for Sonic fans, or simply fans of the 2D platformer genre.

Jotun: Valhalla Edition

Price $14.99
Developed By Thunder Lotus Games
Published By Thunder Lotus Games
Genre Action / Adventure
Rating T
Players 1

Jotun: Valhalla Edition is a beautifully hand-drawn action game that immerses you in Norse mythology. Thora, a female viking warrior who has suffered an inglorious death, must prove herself to the Gods in order to enter Valhalla. Featuring nine levels filled to the brim with Viking mythology, take down ferocious Jotuns (giant Norse elementals) with Thora’s two-handed axe and powers bestowed upon her by the gods. While the game has been on many consoles (including Wii U), the game’s polish and unique premise is certainly cause for more notoriety. Jotun: Valhalla Edition is a beautifully rendered action game that is as challenging as it is beautiful.

Published By Sometimes You
