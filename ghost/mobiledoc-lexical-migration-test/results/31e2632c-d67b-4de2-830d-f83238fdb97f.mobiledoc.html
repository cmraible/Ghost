What exactly is a 1997 Porsche 993 “ Reimagined 4RS” by Porsche Classic Monterey? This luxury sports car was built to promote the classic partnership of factory-trained technicians at Porsche Monterey. It has just over 100 miles on the build since completion, and is begging for a new owner to enjoy it.

In the pursuit of the perfect air-cooled 911, the 1997 Carrera 4S underwent a complete and comprehensive nut-and-bolt RS transformation, making sure to use 97-percent Porsche Classic parts.

The outside appearance was enhanced with front and rear spoilers, and it rides on a capable and overhauled suspension. Moving the car is a 3.8-liter engine. The all-wheel-drive performance car has a lot to admire.

Starting from the inside out, when you slide into this completely redone RS cabin, you'll appreciate the all-new RS carpeting with insulation, the trim, labels, and also all-new leather RS door panels. The rear seats were removed for sporty weight savings, and it got a new 993 RS steering wheels, RS shift knob, red seatbelts, Rennline stainless steel door sills, and highly custom hardback Recaro Sport seats.

The car made its debut at the 2nd annual Porsche Classic Monterey event in 2019 and was admired by many. It has been serviced regularly as carried out by Porsche Monterey since 2008, according to the Carfax report.
