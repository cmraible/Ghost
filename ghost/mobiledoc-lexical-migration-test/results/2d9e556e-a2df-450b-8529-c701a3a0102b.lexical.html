Overview

Serious about performance in 1969, Plymouth was upgrading the Barracuda as a serious contender to be reckoned with.

Serious about performance in 1969, Plymouth was upgrading the Barracuda as a serious contender to be reckoned with. Boosting engine horsepower to 330 on the popular 383 V8 engine option, Plymouth also introduced a cosmetic package built around the Formula S option, which they called the 'Cuda.
