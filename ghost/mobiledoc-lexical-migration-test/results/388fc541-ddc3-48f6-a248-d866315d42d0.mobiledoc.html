The Plymouth Road Runner is one heck of a muscle car. This mid-size Mopar came out swinging in the late 1960s, and has absolutely exploded in popularity over the years. While the moniker is no longer seen in the modern Mopar muscle car lineup, there are still some slick examples out there, and that's what we're looking at today.
