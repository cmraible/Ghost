This reminds us of another bedazzled vehicle here.

And this is why social media has a negative effect on society.

And just what kind of over-the-top wedding will she demand?

As one astute Instagram user noted: “No need to buy so (sic) expensive car just to prove the love. Love is not made by money.” Truer words have never been said. While we certainly wouldn’t kick a 718 Spyder out of our garage, we also don’t advocate for anyone buying such a supercar to impress their girlfriend or social media followers.
