Here is our guide for all of the Gold Boots Parts in order to get the Gold Boots. So, let’s get into it…

But first, here are the guides for the other Gold Parts in case you’re going to need them as well:

#1

For the very first of the Gold Boots Parts, head just west of the Village of Lupia and into Green Fields.

When you get to here, drop down into the hole…

And in that hole is a chest that contains the piece… simple!

 

#2

In this frame, you will need a boomerang.

Take the lillypad down to the bottom (make sure to jump off before you hit the spikes. I didn’t… and they hurt). Make your way to the right and throw that boomerang to collect the second Gold Bracelet Part.

 

#3

The third Gold Boots Part is during the main quest in The lost Temples. You’ll find them in a chest in the second maze.

When you head to the right a bit, you’ll need to jump up to the ledge above, transform into the pig and throw some bombs at the blocks.

Make sure that you have the Prince Armor equipped so that you can see better.

Make your way to the far right and in that room you’ll find a chest, but watch out for the slimes that will appear.

In the chest is one of the Gold Boots Parts… woo!

 

#4

The next Gold Boots Part is in The Volcano, in the top-left corner of the main room where the massive stone tablet is.

To solve this puzzle, we need to look at the big stone tablet after it has been revealed a few times. Do you see the lava fountains in the top-left corner of it. Yep, that’s the order that it needs to be. Don’t worry, it took me a while to figure it out. Basically, ground-pound the one on the far-left twice, the second one once, the third one not at all and the fourth one three times.

 

#5

For this one, head to this frame…

From the platform with the cannon, jump up-left onto the platform that is protruding out of the wall.

There is actually an invisible wall there with a chest that contains what we’re looking for. Simple yet frustrating, right?

 

So now we have all the Gold Boots Parts, we can take them to Foximus in the frame below who will make them into the Golden Boots!
