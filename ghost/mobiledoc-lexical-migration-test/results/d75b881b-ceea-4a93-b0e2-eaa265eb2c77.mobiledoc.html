Gaming on a budget? Well, look no further! You don’t have to spend $60 on every game you buy, there are some fantastic cheap Switch games for under $5 just waiting to be bought and played; from platformers, to puzzle, to party games!

12 Orbits

Price $1.99
Developed By Roman Uhlig
Published By Roman Uhlig
Genre Party / Action / Adventure / Sports
Rating E
Players 2 – 12

12 Orbits is the perfect game to bring out at a party. The game supports up to 12 players at once and you don’t even need any addition controllers. With four gameplay modes, each player with just one finger per player, 12 Orbits is easy to pick up but hard to put down.
