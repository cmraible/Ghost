Overview

The 1975 DeVille was met with styling changes like dual rectangular headlamp lenses flanked by rectangular cornering lights wrapped around the body.

The 1975 DeVille was met with styling changes like dual rectangular headlamp lenses flanked by rectangular cornering lights wrapped around the body. A new cross-hatched grille also appeared. Sedan DeVilles now featured slim triangular quarter windows that mimicked the coach windows that appeared on Coupe DeVilles the previous year.

A new 500ci V8 became the standard engine on DeVille's and had an output of 210 hp and 380 lb-ft of torque.

The DeVille d'Elegance returned which was mainly a luxury-oriented package on the already luxurious DeVille line. It featured new Manhattan, a two-toned crushed velour in Dark Blue, Rosewood, Crimson, and Saddle, and Cadillac claimed it to come with softer leather cushion bolsters.
