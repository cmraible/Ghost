Overview

The big news for the 1965 ‘Vette was the addition of standard 4-wheel disc brakes and an optional, beefier, big-block 396ci/425hp V8.

The big news for the 1965 ‘Vette was the addition of standard 4-wheel disc brakes and an optional, beefier, big-block 396ci/425hp V8.

Styling changes were minimal with new optional standard side exhaust pipes, functional front fender louvers, new wheel covers, and a restyled grille.

Air conditioning, power steering, leather, among other options were now offered.
