Overview

The 1954 DeVilles had lower, sleeker bodies, new cellular grille insert, inverter gull-wing front bumpers, and tapered dagmar style bumper guards.

The 1954 DeVilles had lower, sleeker bodies, new cellular grille insert, inverter gull-wing front bumpers, and tapered dagmar style bumper guards.

Coupe DeVille scripts were seen on the rear corner pillars of the luxury hardtop, which also had wider sill moldings.

Power was up 20 from 210 to 230 horsepower. Spec torque remained the same.

Production was up from 14.550 to 17,170.
