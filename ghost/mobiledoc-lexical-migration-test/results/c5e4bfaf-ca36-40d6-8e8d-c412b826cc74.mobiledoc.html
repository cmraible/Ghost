What is a daily poster?

In the age of the Internet, we are all daily posters — we post, we comment, we retweet, we email, we text, and sometimes we even reply all.

Being a daily poster is not a publishing schedule. It is an ethos, a way of life and a code. We live on the Internet, and when we have something to say, we post it online. That is our commitment to you.

Why become a supporting subscriber to The Daily Poster?

We started this journalism project because we believe in building a new kind of media outlet — one that is reader-supported and independent, which allows us to do the kind of hard-hitting reporting that corporate-funded media often avoids. When you become a supporting subscriber, you are helping fund this project and enabling us to build out our team.
