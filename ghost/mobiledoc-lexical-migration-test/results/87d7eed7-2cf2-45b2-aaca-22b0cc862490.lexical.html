Whomever is lucky enough to buy this 1963 Chevrolet Corvette from RK Motors will receive the NCRS Shipping Data Report and the various award certificates to display with pride. You don’t come across many split-window C2 Corvettes, let alone one in such beautiful condition with a string of impressive awards under its belt.
