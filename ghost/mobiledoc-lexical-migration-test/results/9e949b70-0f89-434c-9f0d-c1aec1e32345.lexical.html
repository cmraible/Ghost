Overview

The 1964 DeVille's styling changed with the new Series 62 pattern. Equipment features remained much the same as the previous year.

The 1964 DeVille's styling changed with the new Series 62 pattern. Equipment features remained much the same as the previous year.

With the new 429ci engine, performance gains were more noticed in the lower range - 20 to 50 mph. Power was increased to 340 hp, but came on much later in the rpm range. What customers were feeling in the lower mph range was the much improved 480 lb-ft of torque!

The new Turbo Hydra-Matic transmission was introduced.

The Park Avenue sedan was discontinued most likely due to very low sales. The model was replaced with a new DeVille 2-dr convertible.

The lineup change, increased output, and new styling all helped increase sales this year.
