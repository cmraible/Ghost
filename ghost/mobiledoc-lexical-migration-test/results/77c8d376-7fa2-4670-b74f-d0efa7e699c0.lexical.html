A big issue that fans had with Fifa 18 was that you were unable to play online with friends, but that appears to have been rectified with Fifa 19.

It is confirmed that players will be able to play with friends in full best of five online friendlies.

The game is also said to be much closer to the PS4/XB1 counterparts, but that’s all that was mentioned on that. It has also been slated for a September release.
