Morning Brew brews up a sale

A newletter startup looks like it's about to be bought by Business Insider. Again, this is something the focus on star journalists with newsletters misses: people have been quietly building successful businesses in this space for years. Morning Brew (the business) is five years old and has 3 million subscribers. But, because they're doing business reporting, rather than mainstream news reporting, we don't get the breathless coverage about it.

As ever, it's much easier to get someone to pay for something when it's the company paying, not their personal credit card. Plenty of scope yet for great B2B newsletters, I think.
