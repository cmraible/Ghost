One Man & His Blog is the personal weblog of Adam Tinworth. I’ve been using it to explore the intersection of digital technology, publishing and journalism for well over 18 years – and it’s where I continue to think out loud as part of my consultancy work.

About Me

I’ve been a journalist for 25 years and a blogger for nearly two decades. Since 2006 I’ve been working-full time in digital journalism and publishing development, and I have extensive experience of change management, workflows, and technology implementation.

I also deliver training of journalists, publishers and other content-centric people from students up to senior management level.

Since 2012, I’ve been working independently as a consultant on:

  • publishing strategy
  • digital journalism
  • mobile
  • social media.

My clients have ranged from international magazine publishers, to national newspapers like the Financial Times and The Telegraph, to small businesses. I’m more interested in the challenge of the work than the size of the client.

I’m also a visiting lecturer at City, University of London where I teach digital journalism and audience engagement skills to Masters students, including video strategy, social media, community management, SEO and audience strategy.

You can see more about my career to date on LinkedIn. If you think I might be able to help you, please drop me a line and we can discuss working together.
