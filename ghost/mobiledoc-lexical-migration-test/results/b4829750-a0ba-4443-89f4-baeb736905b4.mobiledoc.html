Corvette enthusiasts are familiar with the name Chip Miller, who founded Carlisle events along with his business partner Bill Miller (no relation). Chip, a proud Corvette enthusiast, has owned over 80 Corvettes. Sadly, Chip passed away in 2004 of amyloidosis, and that's when his wife Judy and partner Bill started the Chip Miller Amyloidosis Foundation in his honor.

One of the biggest events in the country, the Spring Carlisle show is the place to be when it comes to collector and classic cars and is a must for any car enthusiast. Corvettes of Carlisle is the largest gathering of 'Vettes in the world, and it brings in about 5,000 of them each year.
