Overview

Little changes were made for the 1960 Bel Air, like a more restrained front end, and double taillights replaced the cat's eyes from the previous year.

Little changes were made for the 1960 Bel Air, like a more restrained front end, and double taillights replaced the cat's eyes from the previous year.

Fuel injection was no longer available, but the 348ci was able to achieve 335 hp with the use of three 2-bbl carburetors, a special cam, and 11:25:1 compression.
