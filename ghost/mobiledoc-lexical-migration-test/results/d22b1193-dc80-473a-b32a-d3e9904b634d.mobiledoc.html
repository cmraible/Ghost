On Wednesday 6/23 at 8pm ET (5pm PT), ALL Daily Poster subscribers will have a chance to talk with two-time U.S. presidential candidate Dennis Kucinich.

Along with running for the Democratic presidential nomination in 2004 and 2008, Kucinich served as mayor of Cleveland from 1977 to 1979 and as a U.S. Representative from Ohio from 1997 to 2013. Kucinich recently released his third book, The Division of Light and Power, detailing his mayoral battle to preserve Cleveland's publicly owned electric utility, and he just announced he is once again running for Cleveland mayor.
