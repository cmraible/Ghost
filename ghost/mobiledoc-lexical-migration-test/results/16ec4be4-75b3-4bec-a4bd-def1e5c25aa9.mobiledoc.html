A Set of Audi R8s

I can name the celebrities I know of who don't have R8s, and it would be quicker than listing off the ones who do - and it makes sense, they're great cars. Jason Statham has two of them in his car collection, one in black and the other white. The Audi R8 is powered by a 430-horsepower V8 that can move the car to 60 mph in 4-seconds.

Jaguar E-Type

A favorite of those with timeless taste and desire to exceed the speed limit, the Jaguar E-Type is a classy automobile. It's unclear whether or not Statham actually owns this Jaguar, reports are conflicting, but he did arrive to the premier of his movie, The Mechanic, in it, so we'll take it.

Lamborghini Murcielago LP

The Lamborghini Murcielago LP Jason Statham drove in The Transporter must have left an impression on him, because he has an orange one in real life. Obviously, this is one of the fastest cars in the star's collection. This Lambo will hit 60 mph in 3.4-seconds, and is one of only 4,000 built during the ten year production run.

Porsche 911 GT2

Statham has been spotted in this Porsche numerous times since 2009. Porsche poured a lot into the 911 GT2. This model can sprint from 0-60 mph in 3.7-second, with a top speed of 204 mph.

Aston Martin DBS

This Aston Martin DBS makes us want a James Bond movie staring Jason Statham. The beautiful retractable roof Aston Martin is powered by a 510-horsepower 6.0-liter V12 engine.

Jaguar XJR

Statham has this seriously powerful Jaguar XJR. Powering the Jag is a supercharged 5.0-liter V8 that puts 543-horsepower and 503 lbs-ft of torque.
