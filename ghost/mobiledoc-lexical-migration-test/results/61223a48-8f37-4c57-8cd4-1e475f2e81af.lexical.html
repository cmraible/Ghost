With a custom frame off restoration, this 1958 B61 Mack Truck is being offered at the Hot August Nights Auction in Reno, Nevada. It features a Ford F350 chassis and all of the modern amenities that come with it, including air conditioning, a tilt steering wheel, and cruise control.
