This American convertible will be auctioned off soon.

With temperatures rising and clear skies prevailing all over, this is the perfect time to celebrate spring. There’s no better way to enjoy the season than behind the wheel of a classic convertible. The Motorious Convertibles Only Auction starts on April 24 and this beautiful 1995 Chevrolet Corvette Indianapolis 500 Pace Car will be crossing the block.

Boasting a mere 273 original miles, there’s really no wonder why this ‘Vette looks so clean in every direction. Just like the car used to pace the Indianapolis 500 back in 1995, this car wears a standout two-tone Dark Purple and White paint job. Red stripes which terminate in white graphics, plus special badging on the rear fenders further punctuate the fact this is not your average Chevy.

The special look of this Corvettecontinues to the interior, where two-tone Black and Dark Purple seats look absolutely perfect. All the factory controls and gauges look and work as if this car just rolled off the factory line. Also running smoothly is the sequential fuel-injected 5.7-liter V8 and 4-speed automatic transmission, if you’re interested in some thrills.

Everything about this 1995 Chevrolet Corvette Indianapolis 500 Pace Car looks exceptional, including the original white convertible top. GM only made 527 of these vehicles, making each one a collectible from the get-go.

Included with this Corvetteis a healthy amount of documentation, helping you to be more confident in your purchase and its long-term investment potential. The successful bidder will receive a copy of the original certificate of origin, the original bill of sale, original payment receipt, odometer insurance disclosure statement, original GM Protection Plan Coverage Agreement Registration, original insurance cards, original window sticker, build sheet, GM invoice, owner’s manual, and a copy of the Summer 1995 Corvette Quarterly Magazine featuring the car on the cover.

If interested in bidding on this car, you need to register now. The Motorious Convertibles Only Auction starts April 24 at 9 am EDT.
