The Mercedes-Benz G-Class was born as a rugged military vehicle, but these days, it's known more for being a luxury SUV. Recent additions to the G-Class lineup such as the 6x6 and 4x4² prove that this SUV still has some serious off-road chops, while this custom 2002 Mercedes-Benz G500 shows that you don't need to spend six figures to get an off-road-ready G-Wagen.

This monster Mercedes has been modified for the trails thanks to a two-inch lift, Bilstein shocks and custom bead-lock wheels wrapped in massive Toyo mud-terrain tires. The seats are wrapped in black Nappa leather, and the dark interior is accented by burl walnut wood trim.
