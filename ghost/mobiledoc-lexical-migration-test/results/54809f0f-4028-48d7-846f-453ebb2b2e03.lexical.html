Overview

The 1976 Monte Carlo featured a new crosshatch grille and vertically mounted rectangular headlamps, along with reshaped taillights.

The 1976 Monte Carlo featured a new crosshatch grille and vertically mounted rectangular headlamps, along with reshaped taillights.

A new 140 hp 305ci V8 became the standard engine. The 454ci V8 was discontinued from the option list this year. The Turbo Hydra-Matic became standard on all Monte Carlos.

The instrument panel and steering wheel featured a new rosewood trim replacing the burled elm of previous years. A new option was a two-tone "Fashion Tone" paint combination.

Production was up again setting a new record for itself.
