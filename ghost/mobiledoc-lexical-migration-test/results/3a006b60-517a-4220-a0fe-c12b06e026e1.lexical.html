Painted in the bright factory hue of Hugger Orange, this Camaro has been freshly restored down to the smallest detail.

When it comes to muscle cars, a 1969 Chevrolet Camaro SS 396 Coupe ranks high on the list of cars that one would dream of parking in the garage, especially one as nice as this example that is heading to our Motorious Spring Classic & Collector Car Online Auction that starts in 3 days.

Sporting a flawless finish in the factory hue of Hugger Orange, this bold Camaro SS was given a fresh meticulous restoration all the way down to the smallest detail. All body lines are laser-straight, and a contrasting black vinyl top was added to protect the roof but also adds a pleasing contrast against the beautiful paint job. A set of American Racing Torq Thrust wheels are bolted on all four corners. Power brakes with front discs brings everything to a halt nicely while power steering makes navigating those turns a breeze.

This classic beauty has a beastly side when that new big-block roars to life. That's right, a numbers-correct 396-cubic-inch V8 engine, the L-89 that comes equipped with aluminum heads, is stuffed inside the bay with an output of 375-horsepower. Power is sent straight to the pavement thanks to a manually shifted M-21 close-ratio 4-speed transmission that moves the 3.73 posi-traction rear-end.

Open the doors to this classic Camaro to an immaculate full SS interior in black that is just as nice as its exterior. It features Strato bucket seats and a floor console that houses the gauge package and Hurst shifter.

Just glancing at the photos, and it's easy to tell that this stunning Camaro was given the attention it deserved during its restoration. Guaranteed to impress the judges at any car show, this beautiful 1969 Chevy Camaro SS 396 is heading to our Motorious Spring Classic & Collector Car Online Auction with a minimum bid of $35,000. This classic will be open to bids in on Tuesday, April 14th, so go ahead and mark those calendars.
