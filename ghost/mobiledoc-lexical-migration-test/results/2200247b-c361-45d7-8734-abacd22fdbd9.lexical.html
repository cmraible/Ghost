This guide covers all five Gold Bracelet Parts that you’ll need in order to forge the Gold Bracelet. But enough talking, let’s just get straight into it…

#1

In this frame, you’ll need to jump and slam down on each bat to make your way across here.

If you fall, don’t worry, there’s a platform that can take you back to the top in order to try again. Run and jump just as the first bat is making its way from left to right. Ground-pound at the last second, propel yourself into the air and follow the pattern until you get the last one (when ground-pounding, you can also keep holding down right in order to move just a fraction more in mid-air). After the third bat, fall to the right, go through the door and collect the first of the Gold Bracelet Parts.

#2

(In the screenshots for this one, you may notice that I’m the dragon at the start and I have a lot of heart pieces and that the chest is already opened, we had an issue with the recording of this one so we doubled back and rerecorded it. You probably won’t have that stuff when you get there)

Now, head to this frame and go down the bottom-left section…

For this part, you’re going to need to be the frog, so transform into it if you haven’t done so already. Use your tongue on the + in the middle to rotate the room anti-clockwise once. You’ll then fall down into the next room.

But we don’t want to be in this room, so defeat the two bombers and a bouncing block will appear. jump on that to propel yourself back up to the room that we were in before…

Now go through the exit to the left…

And flick the switch so that a + appears in the middle of that room…

Now head back to the room that we were just in again and rotate the room 180 degrees. Then use the bow to propel yourself upwards.

In this room, rotate the room clockwise just once. Now proceed out to the left.

Defeat the bomber in this room and a + will appear in the middle. Transform into the snake and cling onto the moss in order to make your way around to the +. Transform back into the frog.

Rotate the room anti-clockwise once and proceed down through the bottom-left exit.

In this room, rotate the room clockwise once. Use the bow to propel yourself up to the top-right section and use your tongue to grab the bouncy block.

Now head to the left so that you’re back down on the ground and spit out the block. Rotate the room anti-clockwise once, grab the bouncy block again and use the other bouncy block to the bottom-left to propel yourself upwards.

Now spit out the bouncy block in the bottom-right corner and bounce on it to propel yourself upwards.

Go up to the next room and take out the bomber. This will make the chest appear that has the Gold Bracelet Part inside. Rotate the room 180 degrees and collect your reward!

#3

For the third of the Gold Bracelet Parts, we’ll want to head to this frame…

Transform into the frog and use your tongue on the bow to the far-left.

Make your way down the ladders on the right, fighting off enemies.

When you get to the bottom of this bit, use your tongue on the bow to the left.

This will propel you up to a chest that contains a Gold Bracelet Part.

#4

This one is probably the most difficult of the Gold Bracelet Parts to find. But we got there in the end, so head to this frame…

You’ll want to have swam down and to the left (just left of the big boulder that we destroyed with a bomb as part of the main plot). If there are rocks in the way, you can use the frog’s dash ability to break through them. Once you’re in, stand on this platform…

There’s actually a hidden door here but when you press up, you’ll simply swim away. So you either want to transform into the pig or equip the Heavy Boots in order to keep you grounded.

Now you can head through the door and in there is a chest that contains the part that we need.

#5

Head to this frame (can be easily warped to if you can).

In the bottom-left corner, you probably wouldn’t have realised that there is a hidden door there…

This puzzle requires you to flick the switch on the right and guide the bomb in order to blow up the blocks in the top-right corner.

When you flick the switch, the bomb will make its way to the left and then be pushed down by the current.

Once it gets pushed down, it’ll bounce off of the bouncy button in the bottom-left corner, then to the one to the right of it and then it’ll go back up. Before the bomb goes back up, you’ll want to strike the middle switch so that the current changes to this…

That will effectively push the bomb to the left and into the bouncy button in the top-left corner. When that bounces back, flick the switch again so that the wind current is gone from the top-right and the bomb can make its way to the explodable blocks.

Now swim up to the newly opened gap and there’s a chest with your reward inside!

So now we have all the Gold Bracelet Parts, we can take them to Foximus in the frame below who will make them into the Golden Bracelet!
