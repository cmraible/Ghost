A (very, very short) Bibliography

  • Nic Newman’s annual Digital Media Trends report is essential reading.
  • Paul Bradshaw’s Online Journalism Handbookis dated now, but it covers some of the basic concepts well.
  • George Brock’s Out of Print is a useful guide to the pressures reshaping how we do journalism
  • Stepping outside journalism, Dave Weinberger’s Everything is Miscellaneous is probably the most useful book I’ve ever in read in helping me get my head in the digital space.
  • Eli Parser’s The Filter Bubble is an essential read if you want to understand how digital technology can shrink your worldview
  • Nick Carr’s The Shallows could be looked on as a challenge for us in journalism: how can we use the internet to combat the general dumbing down of information we’re seeing now?
