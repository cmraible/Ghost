1. Biden Offers Amazon Workers Rhetoric, But No Action

On Wednesday, President Joe Biden made headlines supporting Amazon workers trying to unionize and declaring, “Amazon, here we come.” But after Amazon-linked campaign cash has flooded into Democratic coffers, the president has refused to follow his own labor task force’s limited unionization recommendations, and he has failed to reinstate an Obama-era rule requiring companies like Amazon to disclose all of their union-busting spending. Such disclosures could help expose the anti-labor tactics of major law firms — including those that previously employed a key member of Biden’s labor task force.

2. Big Tech's Antitrust Ad Blitz

A misleading multi-million dollar ad campaign reveals how the tech industry is leveraging American consumerism and need for instant gratification in order to oppose landmark antitrust legislation that would make it easier for people and small businesses to sell products and services online.
