Who's ready to take this Blazer on a run?

Originating in 2005, Restore A Muscle Car, has quickly rose as a trusted source for not only the nations restoration needs but the worlds as well. Time and time again they have taken classic muscle from the brink of disarray to stunning works of art, the likes of which never rolled off a dealers lot.

Although they specialize in classic muscle their expertise doesn’t stop there. They have built everything from antique trucks to modern muscle and everything in between and their latest build is a definite show stopper. Taking care to preserve what makes classic Blazers so desirable, the Restore A Muscle Car team gave a 1972 Chevrolet K5 Blazer a face lift and brought it into the modern era.

The restoration of this 1972 Chevrolet K5 Blazer started with an Astro Blue paint job for the original sheet metal and developed into a full fledged frame-off restoration. The team went above and beyond with the interior, utilizing new Houndstooth seating, a Vintage Air AC system, Dakota Digital electronics and gauges, and a Boss touch screen head unit. This equates to a fully customizable custom interior that is sure to suit even the most pampered of drivers.

Power is more than adequate thanks to a new 5.3-liter Vortec fuel injected V8 backed by a three speed automatic transmission. The BFG 305/65R17 all terrain tires wrapped around 17”x9” steel wheels aid the 4.10 gears, rear locker, and NP-205 transfer case ensuring the truck can go pretty much anywhere. Whether on or off road this 1972 K5 Blazer 4x4 has got it covered.
