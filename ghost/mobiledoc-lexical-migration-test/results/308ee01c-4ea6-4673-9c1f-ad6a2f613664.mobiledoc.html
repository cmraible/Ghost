For this week’s Switch Kickstarter Project, we are going to be highlighting the Pro Ergonomic Grips for Nintendo Switch.

These Pro Ergonomic Grips are designed to replicate the feeling of a regular controller when either playing in handheld mode or when you’re playing with the Joy-Cons detached.

The project requires AU$4,182 to be funded and it is currently sitting at AU$1,111 with 32 days remaining. The project is expected to ship in March 2019.
