This week features a total of 11 games on the Switch, including the critically-acclaimed Outlast 2.

So let’s get right into it…

 

Note: All prices are in AUD and all release dates are for the Australian eShop. Also, releases can still be subjected to change after the release of this update.

 

Outlast 2

Release Date 27/03/18

Price $39.95

Developed By Red Barrels

Published By Red Barrels

Genre Action / Adventure

Required Space 9.6 GB

Players 1

Also Released On PC / Mac / PS4 / Xbox One

In Outlast 2, we are introduced to Sullivan Knoth who has created Temple Gate, a town deep in the wilderness and is away from civilisation. You play as Blake Langermann who has found himself in the thick of the mayhem.

 

Warp Shift

Release Date 28/03/18

Price $15.00

Developed By Isbit Games

Published By Isbit Games

Genre Puzzle

Required Space 794 MB

Players 1

Also Released On iOS / Android

Warp Shift is a unique puzzle game that relies on you to challenge your mind and stimulate your senses. Guide Pi and her magical companion through cube-based mazes. With no time limit across five worlds (15 levels in each), take your time and enjoy the picturesque visuals and beautiful soundtrack.

 

The Charming Empire

Release Date 29/03/18

Price $32.50

Published By D3 Publisher

Genre Adventure

Required Space 515 MB

Players 1

Also Released On PC / iOS / Android

This dating simulation game is set in the Taisho Roman era of Japan. Amane Kosaka was living in a countryside until she was suddenly summoned by her brother and ruler, Soshi Amazaki, to live in the empire. With a brand new world to explore that is completely foreign to her, she must face the challenges that lay ahead.

 

The Adventures of Bertram Fiddle: Episode 1: A Dreadly Business

Release Date 29/03/18

Price $7.99

Published By Chorus Worldwide Games

Genre Adventure / Puzzle

Required Space 986 MB

Players 1

Also Released On PC / iOS

This quirky game is packed full of three things: intrigue, mystery and terrible puns. With the visuals from Rumpus Animation, this game takes place in Victorian London and is inhabited by strange characters with even stranger noses. Lead Bertram Fiddle in a point-and-click and held him follow the clues and track down the elusive serial killer known as Geoff the Murderer (makes sense). With over 30 locations to explore featuring beautifully animated interactions, this may be a game to keep on your radar.

 

Toki Tori

Release Date 30/03/18

Price $7.50 (Pre-Order 10% Off: $6.75)

Developed By Two Tribes

Published By Two Tribes Publishing

Genre Puzzle / Platformer

Required Space 127 MB

Players 1

Also Released On PC / Linux / Mac / PS4 / Wii U

With over 80 levels spread over five worlds to explore, this puzzle platformer relies on your creative thinking. Control a not-so athletic chicken with an arsenal of tools at his disposal, including the Telewarp, Freeze-o-Matic and InstantRock.

 

Shadow Bug

Release Date 30/03/18

Price $13.50

Published By Muro Studios

Genre Platformer / Action / Adventure / Puzzle

Required Space 809 MB

Players 1

Also Released On PC / iOS / Android

Shadow Bug features beautifully melancholy artwork in a platform adventure. Play as the ultimate ninja through 36 hand crafted levels that require you to move only by attacking enemies.

 

ClusterPuck 99

Release Date 30/03/18

Price $12.99

Published By Coatsink Software

Genre Party / Arcade / Sports

Required Space 234 MB

Players 1 – 8

Also Released On PC / Xbox One

ClusterPuck 99 is a top-down competitive sports game that you can play with your friends and family. It can be played with up to 8 players across 30 maps and is perfect for players of all ages. Bash your opponents to gain control of the puck and then fight your way to their goal whilst avoiding spikes and using speed boosts.

 

Gotcha Racing 2nd

Release Date 30/03/18

Price $13.00

Developed By Arc System Works

Published By Arc System Works

Genre Racing / Action

Required Space 459 MB

Players 1 – 4

Also Released On 3DS

Customize your own vehicle with parts that you get from gacha machines (featuring approximately 700 different parts!). Win races and aim for the top of the Grand Prix with your custom designed little racer. There are 16 different courses and solo and multiplayer game modes.

 

ACA NeoGeo Sengoku 3

Alteric

Spartan

Release Date 01/04/18

Price $16.50

Published By Sinister Cyclops

Genre Platformer / Arcade / Action

Required Space 725 MB

Players 1

Also Released On PC / PS4 / Xbox One

Set in Sparta, 500 BC, play as King Leo, seek vengeance on those responsible for the magical portals appearing throughout ancient Greece and recover everything that was stolen. Spartan features over 20 hours of intense gameplay that spans across four distinct worlds and 24 challenging levels.
