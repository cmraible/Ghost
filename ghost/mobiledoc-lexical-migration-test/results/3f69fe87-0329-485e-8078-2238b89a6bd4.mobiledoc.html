The @evleaks Twitter account just posted two photos on Twitter, one includes the possible specs for the Galaxy S IV, which align with our previous posts and the other is a rendering that shows the supposed Galaxy S IV alongside its predecessors.  The specs list reads: Super AMOLED Full HD touch screen (but doesn’t specify a size), 16GB/32GB/64GB storage options with 2GB of RAM, a 13MP camera with LED Flash, and Android 4.2 (Jelly Bean).

While we can’t verify if these are the final specs or designs, we’ve included both images for your viewing pleasure.  As a reminder the Samsung event is on March 14 at 7PM in New York where the truth will be revealed.

Update: Turns out, it’s not the real deal, just some designers mockup based on no inside information.
