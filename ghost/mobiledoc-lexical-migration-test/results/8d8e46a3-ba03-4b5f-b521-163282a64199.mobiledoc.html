Overview

The 1968 Nova was fully redesigned on a longer 111-inch wheelbase, which was only one inch shorter than Chevy's midsize Chevelle.

The 1968 Nova was fully redesigned on a longer 111-inch wheelbase, which was only one inch shorter than Chevy's midsize Chevelle.

There were a few Novas built with the 194ci, although not listed in the Drivetrain section.

The station wagon and hardtop sport coupe were discontinued.

The Nova Super Sport was transformed from a trim option to a performance package for 1968. It could be had with a 295 hp 350ci up to a 375 hp 396ci. Along with the heavier engines came heavy-duty suspension and "rock crusher" transmission.
