Overview

Very little changed for the 1974 Charger from the previous year. A revised grille, larger rubber bumpers, and taillights summed up the changes.

Very little changed for the 1974 Charger from the previous year. A revised grille, larger rubber bumpers, and taillights summed up the changes. The Charger Rallye was still an option and included all the standard Charger features plus front and rear sway bars, power bulge hood, Rallye instrument cluster, body tape stripes, hood pins, and special exterior badging. The Charger SE was still the top trim level. This year would be the last year of the third-generation Charger, and they would continue on their way to being a luxury car as the muscle car era had faded.
