You simply can’t beat the feel of this Porsche…

Porsche is one of those brands whose reputation precedes it exponentially in terms of performance. Their style combined with a laundry list of accomplishments on the racing circuit have made it quite formidable in that area. All of this culminated to a climax somewhere in the late 1980s and 1990s, particularly with their 911 series cars. One such example could be this 1989 Porsche 911 Carrera coupe G 50 but that should be something that you could decide yourself. After all, you might just end up being the next driver.

First let’s talk about why you might want to do that, a question that is often forgotten when talking about Porsches because of their name. To put it simply, this is a driver‘s car which was built with the specific purpose of severely enriching its operator's life. This is very clearly shown with the G 55 speed manual transaxle which gives the driver full control over the car's acceleration and handling capabilities. Without a doubt, it’s a gearbox that truly lives up to its reputation among car people across the nation.

Then there is the motor which spins the tires of this automotive masterpiece. Utilizing 3.2-liters of displacement in one of their most iconic engine configurations, this engine is bonkers. Of course, you can’t really beat the sound of a thumping flat-six powertrain from one of Germany’s premium track car producers. Overall, this Porsche is a wonderful combination of style and speed with heritage unlike anything else. That’s exactly why you should consider it for your automotive collection.
