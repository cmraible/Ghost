Go ahead and make 'em green with envy.

When people think back to the muscle car era, the Chevelle SS is one of the first to come to mind with its aggressive muscle car looks and big block power. Adventure Classics is offering the opportunity to own this stunning 1969 Chevrolet Chevelle SS.

Sprayed in a factory correct 1969 color of Fathom Green, the exterior of this Chevelle is in fantastic shape. Body panels have perfect fitment and the chrome bumpers shine like new. Exterior highlights include all SS badging, factory correct SS 396 stripe, a blacked-out grille and tail panel, and twin hood power bulges. A set of chrome 5-spoke Mag wheels sit on all four corners and are wrapped with black wall performance tires.

Under the hood sits a period-correct 396-cubic-inch big block V8 Chevy engine that is rated at 325-horsepower from the factory. Painted in the factory shade of Chevy Orange, the engine is equipped with a Holley four-barrel carburetor, aluminum intake manifold, and longtube headers that send spent gases through a dual exhaust. A chrome open air cleaner and chrome valve covers add to the engine bay aesthetic. The Chevelle also features performance spark plug wires, coil, and distributor. Power steering allows for maneuvering corners with ease, and power brakes bring everything to a halt.

Open the doors to a matching green interior that features a split bench front seat and rear seat in green vinyl upholstery. More interior highlights include the column-mounted shifter, upgraded wood 3-spoke sports steering wheel, full factory gauge panel, accessory gauges, and an AM/FM radio. Green carpeting has been laid throughout the cabin.
