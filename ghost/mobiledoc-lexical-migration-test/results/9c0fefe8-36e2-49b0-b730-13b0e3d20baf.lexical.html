Overview

The Ford Thunderbird had done everything Ford intended for it to do. It captured the hearts of America, brought people into Ford showrooms so they could see it first hand and marvel at the craftsmanship of this fine automobile.

The Ford Thunderbird had done everything Ford intended for it to do. It captured the hearts of America, brought people into Ford showrooms so they could see it first hand and marval at the craftsmanship of this fine automobile.

1957 would be the third and final year for the original two passenger Thunderbird, and some feel the third year was the best one yet. After 57 Ford would do away with the two seater sporty personal car.

Ford applied attractive styling updates that addressed some of the issues with the former models, and sales grew to 21,380. Rear fins appeared on the 57 which was becoming popular by automakers.
