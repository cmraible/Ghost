With the top down, the completely new red leather interior is on full display. The factory heater and air conditioning work, and all the factory controls as well as gauges are included. A new sound system has been installed, complete with two JL Audio amplifiers and JL Audio W6 subwoofers mounted in the trunk, so you can listen to your favorite tunes.

If you’re afraid this car is going to scrape bottom on driveway skirts, speed bumps, or just about anything else, there’s a provision for that. A RideTech adjustable air ride suspension allows you as the driver to rise the ride height as needed, then drop it back for smooth cruising.

To get this long car moving you need something powerful, and the 502ci GM Performance Big Block Crate V8 with 4bbl carb definitely qualifies for the job. This means you won’t struggle getting up the steepest hills or hitting freeway speeds on the on-ramp in this amazing ride.

Cadillac made the Series 62 from 1940 to 1964, and it was one of the most recognizable models to come out of the golden age of American car designs. The dramatic looks of the 1960 Series 62 came about for the 1959 model year when Cadillac designers added those large tail fins and rear fender skirts, creating a look which is still iconic today.
