Enter FaceStack

Substack and newsletters become a big thing, so clearly the Zuckercopier has been fired up to clone the product. Facebook has provided a little more detail on its forthcoming newsletter/creator monetisation offering.

While I can see why Facebook is doing this, and why it might seem like an attractive idea to do this from some creators' point of view, the idea of the whole — or even a substantial chunk — of my income being dependent on Facebook's good will brings me out in hives.

I see no sign here that Facebook will make it easy to move your subscribers, and their billing details, if you ever moved platform. Risky.
