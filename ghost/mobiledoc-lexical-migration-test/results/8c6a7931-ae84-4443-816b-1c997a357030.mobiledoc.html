Welcome to part 2 of our walkthrough of the Pikuniku Adventure Mode! Whilst this game is quite linear, there are still some moments and puzzles that may leave you scratching your head. So, let’s get into it…

Main Objective: Explore the world

Now that you’re in the forest, make your way to the right for some cutscene goodness. Once that is done…

Main Objective: Help the stuck villagers 1/2

Continue right until you get to here…

Jump onto the cog and when you stand on it, it will turn. Make sure that you’re facing right and the bottom platform will rotate clockwise. We’re going to want to rotate the bottom platform until it gets to this point and rotate the bottom-middle platform until it gets to this point…

Jump onto the left cog and then jump upwards so that you’re on top of the log.

Jump over to the next platform and then onto the top-middle cog. Rotate it so that you can jump onto the next platform and then onto the next cog.

Rotate that cog so that you can jump onto the platform just to the right, then jump onto the L shaped platform. To get to the villager, kick the plank.

Main Objective: Help the stuck villagers 2/2

When you drop down and continue right through the forest, another villager will destroy the big boulder with an exploding pinecone. Keep heading right and you’ll see the second villager in need of your help. To get to them, jump onto the tree branch and let it bounce and propel you upwards.

Head up the trees by using the branches and head right. Rotate the platform by using the cog here and continue onwards.

Here, you’ll want to rotate the first cog so that the first platform looks like this…

Jump onto the platform and then onto the cog. Rotate it so that the next platform is situated like this…

Now use the platforms to make your way up to the L shaped platform, kick the plank down and there you have it, you’ve saved both of the villagers!

Main Objective: Go to the meeting point

Jump down and proceed to the right of screen. You’ll come across another cutscene with more explosive pinecone goodness. Once that’s done, we’ll want to head to the meeting point, but we’ll need the card first. For this, you’ll want to head into the Sunshine Club, however the robot will scan you and judge whether you have enough style.

Main Objective: Get swaggy

The robot won’t let you in until you have bought and equipped the sunglasses. but lucky for us, the shop is right below the club. Head there and buy the sunglasses for just one coin – what a bargain!

I thought I looked great as the flower, but whatever…

Head back to the club and equip the sunglasses. Now that you’re looking swaggy, the robot guard will let you in.

Main Objective: Challenge the robot to a dance contest

After the short cutscene, talk to the robot in the middle of the dance floor in front of the speakers. And now… we dance!

All you need to do is press the correct button in time with the music. You’ll just need to beat your opponent’s score of 64% – simple!

Once that’s done, speak to the rebel just to the right of the stage and he’ll give you the magnetic card.

Head out of the club back to the forest and fall down. Now, we can unlock this gate with the magnetic card…

Proceed to talk to the rebels down there to learn more about what is happening. Once you’ve gone through the dialogue, it’s time to…

Main Objective: Beat the giant robot of the forest

Head back out and up to the forest. Proceed right, jump onto the trees that have been cut down and kick the giant robot.

This will initiate the boss fight. The aim of this fight is to avoid the circular saw. The robot will suddenly drop its saw on either the left or right side and will slide it along the ground, so your job is to jump over it. The trick to figuring out which side the robot is going to drop its saw is by watching its eyes. Either the left or right robot will duck its head for a second, almost as if it’s winking at you.

After avoiding the saw twice, kick the explosive pinecone that the rebel on the right throws down to you. This will hit the robot and do damage to it.

Repeat that process two more times (You’ll only need to avoid the saw once for these rounds) and victory will be yours! After the fight, the rebels will bring you back to the hideout.
