The indie hit, The Messenger, is a fantastic game and it’s about to get even better with free the free DLC pack, Picnic Panic, available right now! Here’s what Devolver Digital had to say:

Sabotage Studio and Devolver Digital released today  Picnic Panic, the FREE sun-soaked expansion to the critically acclaimed retro platformer The Messenger, on Nintendo Switch, PlayStation 4 and PC. The Messenger was also added to the Xbox Game Pass for PC library, and is up to 33% off on all platforms so give into our pier-pressure and SEAS THE GAME!
 
Not only are the beloved characters from the original game tagging along on your trip to Voodkin Island, but you’ll be able to explore three refreshingly new levels – each culminating in unique and inventive boss fights. As they say, keep your friends close, and your anemones closer.
 
If that’s not enough to entice you into our tropical getaway, Rainbowdragoneyes has provided 13 brand new tracks for your Voodkin Vacation playlist. Shell yeah.
