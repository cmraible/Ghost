Overview

The 1979 Corvette did see some slight changes including a standard AM/FM radio, and increased horsepower in both available engines due to a new dual snorkel intake.

The 1979 Corvette did see some slight changes including a standard AM/FM radio, and increased horsepower in both available engines due to a new dual snorkel intake.

The bolt-on rear spoilers from the ’78 Pace Car were now available as an option and reduced drag by approximately 15%. The automatic equipped cars had the rear gear ratio changed to 3:55:1. Last year for the “Corvette” named colors and production was at its highest ever.
