Welcome to TechieBytes episode 85 titled How this Miami Ag-tech startup is upgrading farming (feat. Lior Barhai)

Today I'm speaking with Lior Barhai, Co-founder & CEO at Envonics, a startup based in Miami that's helping farmers using hydroponics grow better and more sustainably with data. We discuss why these improvements in farming are necessary, how they lead to a healthier population, why he's building in Miami, and more. Enjoy!
