It hasn’t even been a month since Super Smash Bros. Ultimate launched worldwide and we are already seeing some crazy sales figures for it. As mentioned in the headline, Super Smash Bros. is dominating the sales charts, beating out heavy hitters like Mario Kart 8 Deluxe (which launched in April, last year) and Super Mario Odyssey (October, last year).

According to sales charts, The only Switch game that has sold more than Super Smash Bros. Ultimate is Splatoon 2 at 2,809,117 copies. However with Super Smash Bros. Ultimate still selling over 450,000 copies a week, it may not be long before it takes the crown.
