The 1958 DeVille was basically a carryover from '57, but with a facelift. The new grille featured multiple round "cleats" at the intersection of horizontal and vertical members. New quad (dual) headlamps illuminated night driving and small chrome fins decorated the front fenders. Tailfins were a bit less pronounced.

There was yet another power gain achieved this year of 10 hp making 310 total hp.

Because of a failing economy and new styling, sales dropped over 5,000 from the previous year.
