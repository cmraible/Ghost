This gorgeous pickup is both beauty and beast.

There's just something about a classic pickup truck that draws you in. Not only does a a vintage truck feature sleek body lines that flow from front to back, but it also exudes toughness. there is also a toughness that comes along with it. This 1969 Chevrolet CST-10 is no exception. This beautiful pickup is hitting the virtual auction stage through GAA Classic Car Auctions on Friday, November 7th.

Back in the day, the CST/10 was the top-of-the-line pickup truck offered in Chevrolet's lineup. Not only did it come with all available options, but it would also haul more than just the mail. It was a desirable truck back in 1969, and that still rings true today.

This beautiful example of a CST/10 was given a professional ground-up restoration in 2015. The exterior is painted in PPG Deep Atlantic Blue paint and then sprayed over with a Pearl clear. Handling is not an issue because it hugs the road with a lowered suspension with the help of power steering. Further complementing the exterior is the addition of 17-inch Billet Specialties Vintec wheels.

Open the engine bay to a professionally assembled Chevy 383cui V8 engine backed by a custom-built 700R4 four-speed transmission with a 2.500-RPM stall. The back is beefed up with a Drivetrain Specialties Dana 60 rear axle, Eaton Truetrac Differential, and 3.73 gears. Open the doors to a black Houndstooth interior complete with Classic Auto air conditioning and a modern audio system.

If you're in the market for a classic pickup truck that is both beauty and beast, then look no further. You can bid on this beauty through GAA Classic Car Auctions, so make your calendars now. You're not going to want to miss the opportunity to wake up everyday knowing this alluring truck is awaiting a joyride. Feel free to make an offer here.
