First-gen Camaros are practically a must-have for any serious collection.

There are almost getting to be too many pro-touring and restomod versions of the first-gen Chevrolet Camaro these days, so if you're looking for a clean, stock example, then check out this amazing 1968 Chevrolet Camaro RS/SS Convertible. This iconic Camaro is up for sale through Corvette Mike Midwest, and it has been fully restored so it's ready to show or cruise.

This Camaro RS/SS might be an older restoration (about eight years ago), but it still looks incredible. That starts with its gorgeous coat of Matador Red paint, which is complemented by the white RS body striping and a black convertible soft top. Being the RS, this Camaro has an unmistakable look with its hidden headlights, while the SS side of the equation packs a 396 CID V8 under the hood. As if this Camaro wasn't desirable enough, the 350-horsepower engine is matched with a four-speed manual gearbox.

Whoever restored this Camaro did an excellent job bringing this car back to its original look right down to the rally wheels and the black and gray houndstooth interior, but some aftermarket touches were added to improve the user experience of this classic. The 396 V8 should sound even better thanks to a Magnaflow exhaust system, and speaking of sound, this car has a modern audio system with Bluetooth capability and ports for both USB and AUX.

While Corvette Mike Midwest obviously specializes in Corvettes, this fully restored 1968 Chevrolet Camaro RS/SS Convertible proves that their selection of non-Corvettes is just as impressive. This stunning Camaro convertible is priced at $56,990, so be sure to contact Corvette Mike Midwest for more information and availability because this rare American icon won't last long.
