Driving a 2020 Porsche 718 Boxster is a great way to fully enjoy the road, but what’s even better is winning one! You can feel good about buying raffle tickets for this auction because all the money is going to an excellent cause. Proceeds will support the University of Kentucky Children’s Hospital. Helping kids who are sick or hurt is always the right thing to do, plus you could win a 2020 Porsche 718 Boxster. As the winner, you get to choose the color of the car’s exterior and interior, so it’s truly yours.

Road-hugging performance and luxurious features make the 2020 Porsche 718 Boxster a dream on wheels. The turbocharged flat-four engine produces a thrilling 300-horsepower, enabling the roadster to do 0-60 in under 5 seconds. With the engine weight sitting low and in the middle of the chassis, this sports car is made to stick the sharpest turns with precision, allowing you to carve up the road or track. On a private course with long enough of a straightaway, you can hit 170 mph!

Germans really know how to make a comfortable interior, as you’ll definitely learn with the 2020 Porsche 718 Boxster. Well-contoured seats, soft leather upholstery, and intuitive controls make driving the roadster a treat. Enhancing things further is the new Porsche Communication Management system. You use a high-resolution touchscreen to play music on the hard drive or through a connected device. Hands-free phone use is also possible through Bluetooth, keeping your hands on the steering wheel.

The drawing for this shiny new 2020 Porsche 718 Boxster will be held Saturday, July 18, 2020 at the Keeneland Concours d’Elegance. Get your tickets now before it’s too late!
