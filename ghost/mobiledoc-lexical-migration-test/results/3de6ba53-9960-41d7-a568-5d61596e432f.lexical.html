Switchaboo’s Pick of the Week

This one wasn’t an easy decision. It is crazy to think that TWENTY-ONE games are coming out on the Switch this week, and there was no way that we could play them all and analyse which game would be the best bang for your buck. However if we had to choose just one out of this long roster, we would have to go with…

Drum roll please…

Blossom Tales: The Sleeping King!

Image Source

This game just simply SCREAMS nostalgia for all classic retro Nintendo fans. There is a reason why it is a console exclusive for the Nintendo Switch.

Blossom Tales: The Sleeping King is such a love letter to the classic The Legend of Zelda: A Link to the Past, and we here at Switchaboo are just such massive fans. The artstyle, the music, the combat, the dungeons… there’s even a Tree Lord! All this just brings back a flood of warm memories that come from a place of love and childhood wonder. It is just such a perfect fit for the Switch.
