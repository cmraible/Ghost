Best Overall Computer Speakers

Bowers & Wilkins MM-1

If you want great sound and a set of computer speakers that don’t take up much room on your desk the Bowers & Wilkins MM-1 speakers are just what you need.  The MM-1 speakers have a very balanced sound, they are not what you would call overpowering in any particular area.  They’re just right, and even as a 2.0 set these speakers produce some nice bass.  Not only do the B&W MM-1 speakers sound amazing, they have a stylish and sleek design and come with a remote, too.

Pricing starts at $499.  Buy the Bowers & Wilkins MM-1 speakers.

 

Klipsch Pro Media 2.1 (Wireless)

If you like your music and movies loud with some really thumping bass, look no farther than the Klipsch Pro Media 2.1 speakers.  This 2.1 set of speakers really pack a punch.  The sound is immersive and crisp.  You will be lost in audio bliss listening to these.  And with a price tag of $199, it’s tough to find a better set of quality 2.1 speakers for such a low price.

Pricing starts at $199.  Buy the Klipsch Pro Media 2.1 (wireless) speakers.

 

Audioengine A2

This set of 2.0 speakers by Audioengine provide quality audio for a reasonable price.  These sleek bookshelf speakers have great bass, solid highs, and good mids, allowing these 60W (30 watts per channel) bad boys to pump out great sound without any distortion.  The A2 speakers are available in both white and back color schemes.

Pricing starts at $199.  Buy the Audioengine A2 speakers.
