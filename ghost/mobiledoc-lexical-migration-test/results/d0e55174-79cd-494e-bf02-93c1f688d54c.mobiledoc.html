Mother/Earthbound series creator Shigesato Itoi has announced on his website that the Hobonichi Shinbun will be publishing the ‘Pollyanna’ Official Mother Comic. The comic is a combined effort from 35 different manga artists/writers, as well as Undertale creator Toby Fox and artists from Nichijou & Pop Team Epic.

And curtesy of YouTuber “Mother Forever”, they have provided an English translated version:

The comic will release in Japan on June 11th for ¥2,640 and sold from the Hobonichi store and two weeks later, will then see a full Japanese release in various retail outlets.

If you haven’t watched the video until the very end, you may have missed it saying “To Be Continued..”, which may point towards even more Mother news later this year.
