Over the past few years, we lost our way. It's tough for me to admit, but we did. There's a multitude of reasons for this but I think the triggering of it all actually revolves around the shut down of my analytics startup, KYA, back at the end of 2017. It was then that the quality of the content we were publishing started to fade until it was not of the caliber that I had held myself and the team to for so many years prior. I'm here to tell you that's no longer going to be the case. Moving forward, you can expect to see a lot more authenticity in our content.

What's new at BestTechie

Here's what I'm super excited to announce starting today.

  2. Implementation of a brand new CMS (we migrated from WordPress to Ghost) and comment system that empowers you, the audience, to engagement on the site in a positive way.
  3. Become a destination for entrepreneurial resources with the launch of the brand new Resources section of the site which will feature a collection of helpful resources ranging from recommended downloads to must-read newsletters and articles to must-listen and watch podcasts and videos. If you have recommendations for resources that you think should be added to the section, let us know!
  4. The launch of our very own membership product: BestTechie Plus which offers access to a number of great perks such as access to our online courses (including early access to our course on how to become a highly effective business networker), an ad-free site experience, access to our member-only Discord to meet and communicate with other people in the community (including myself!), as well as access to member-only discounts and giveaways, plus much more.   5. We're relaunching the TechieBytes podcast too, starting with two brand new episodes that are being released today. Additionally, we're always looking for new and interesting guests, if you or someone you know would be a good guest on the podcast, please get in touch.

What else you can expect from BestTechie

I mentioned authenticity being critical for BestTechie — I believe that (especially with everything that's going on in the world right now as well as over the past few years) authenticity is what's going to help us move forward both at BestTechie and in general. No one's perfect and we can no longer pretend to live in a siloed tech "bubble." My commitment to you is that all future BestTechie content will be what I'm calling: high quality, but not [necessarily] perfect. We will only publish content we feel really good about – this means we may not publish daily (at least, at first) but you can rest assured when we do hit that publish button it'll be worth checking out. I encourage you to take the same mindset into your own work too. I've found it to be extremely helpful.
