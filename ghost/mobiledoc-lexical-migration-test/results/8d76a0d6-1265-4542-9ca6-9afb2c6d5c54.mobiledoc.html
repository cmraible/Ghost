This spectacular build will be shown at the 2022 Deuce’s Wild Competition car show.

Project “Low Touring” is a 1962 Chevy Impala one impressive classic. It’s owned by Brad Nye and built by Jesse Barratt & Jeff Manzella of Blue Sky Performance. It has not been seen or posted on social media, and the exclusive reveal will take place at the 2022 Deuce’s Wild Competition Car Show.

Here are the specs:

  • Roadster shop SPEC chassis on bags
  • Forgeline LS3 wheels
  • Baer brakes
  • Design and concept by Brian Stupski
  • Custom 1 off body parts. (grille, bumper inserts, rear body molding)
  • Pancaked trunk lid.
  • Flip down taillights for battery charger and fuel fill.
  • Belly pan underside.
  • 840hp Blue Print engines supercharged LS3
  • Bux Custom interior
  • Smoked frameless glass
