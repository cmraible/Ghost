Overview

The 1967 DeVille experienced minor trim variations and slightly richer interiors. Tiffany style chrome signature scripts were again found above the body side molding on the rear fenders.

The 1967 DeVille experienced minor trim variations and slightly richer interiors. Tiffany style chrome signature scripts were again found above the body side molding on the rear fenders.

DeVille equipment was the same as the Calais models plus power operated window regulators, rear cigarette lighters in all styles, and tow-way power front seats. Also, an innovative slide-out fuse box and safety front seat back lock for two-door models were additional advances for Cadillac this year.

Engine output remained the same as the previous two years and sales were slightly down.
