Win this 760-horsepower powerhouse from the Shelby American collection complete with Carbon Fiber Track Pack.

Ford proved they were armed with a heavy hitter when they finally introduced the highly anticipated 2020 Shelby GT500 Mustang. Straight out of the factory gates, this high-performance Mustang was given potent power paired with an attractive - yet aggressive - appearance. In fact, this is Ford's most track-precise Mustang to date. Shelby American is offering the chance to take home an instant collectible in the form of a 2020 Shelby GT500.

Equipped with the Carbon Fiber Track Pack, the already limited-production Shelby is even more rare. Exterior-wise, the car is painted in Ford Performance Blue complete with painted "over the top" stripes. A set of 20-inch exposed carbon fiber wheels have been fitted to all four corners and come wrapped with Ford Performance-spec Michelin Sport Cup 2 rubber. Also included in the carbon fiber Track Pack is a carbon fiber instrument panel and adjustable GT4 track wing as part of the carbon fiber package.

Powering the car is a supercharged 5.2-liter cross plane crank V8 that generates 760-horsepower and 625 lb/ft of torque. A MagneRide damping system makes up the advanced suspension and keeps handling in check and power planted to the pavement. Massive powerful Brembo brakes bring all power to a halt with ease. This track-oriented Shelby shifts via a dual-clutch.

On top of an incredible and rare 2020 Shelby GT500 Ford Mustang, the winner also receives a $1,000 gift for some top-of-the-line cleaning products from Adam's Polishes. Even more, the winner gets to attend GT500 Track Attack experience at Ford's Performance Racing School.

Each donation enters you into the drawing, and money goes toward supporting the nonprofit Shelby American Collection museum in Boulder, Colorado. The museum is dedicated to the history and the larger-than-life career of Carroll Shelby and his Shelby American Team.
