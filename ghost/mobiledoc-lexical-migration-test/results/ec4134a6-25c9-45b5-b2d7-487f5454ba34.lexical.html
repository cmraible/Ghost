Finally – BBC Video is becoming embeddable. It’s been a while in coming [because of rights issues](http://www.bbc.co.uk/blogs/bbcinternet/2009/03/embedding_in.html), apparently, and it’s still only running on a limited scale – the technology section only – but it’s a nice step in the right direction, and should see BBC content more directly used as a trigger point for conversation – and isn’t that what all journalists want?
