The 1963 Nova featured a new grille insert and additional trim.

The Nova 400 Convertible and Sports Coupe now offered an SS package sporting SS emblems, a silvery taillight panel, and bright side molding. Standard engine for the SS was still a 6-cylinder, although many ended up with V8s under the hood as it proved a mighty combination of power and light-weight for many drag racers.

Engines and power output remained identical to the previous year.
