Welcome to TechieBytes episode 72 titled Disrupting the male-dominated eSports industry (feat. Avori Henderson)

Today I’m speaking with Avori Henderson, professional gamer and model, she recently hosted an all-female PUBG tournament that garnered more than 135k unique views and raised over $5,000 for charity. We discuss the state of the eSports industry, how the pandemic has affected gaming, what it’s like to be a professional gamer, how to handle and deal with trolls, and so much more. Enjoy!
