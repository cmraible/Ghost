The Subscriptions Boom

I'm not going to surprise anyone here by saying that this has been a rough couple of months for media businesses. The super short version is that there are three major streams of revenue for media companies right now:

  • Ads
  • Events
  • Subscriptions

The pandemic has brutalised two of those for the time being - and so, attention has shifted to the third: subscriptions. For some, the news has been good.

There's a lot more information on the subject in the INMA report by Grzegorz Piechota, which I was lucky enough to edit. It's a fascinating read, that explores in detail exactly which readers are becoming subscribers - and how you might go about retaning them.
