On Tuesday 11/30 at 7 pm ET (4 pm PT), Daily Poster subscribers spoke with the stars of MELTDOWN, Sirota’s new hit podcast series that explores how the 2008 financial crisis and the Obama administration’s tepid response to it helped create the conditions for the rise of Donald Trump, and how Democrats risk repeating the same history today.

Subscribers can find a link below to a replay of the event.

Sirota was joined by a series of guests featured prominently in the podcast series: Brad Miller, former Democratic Congressman from North Carolina; John Nichols, correspondent for The Nation; Neil Barofsky, former inspector general of the Troubled Asset Relief Program; and Lisa Epstein, a nurse turned mortgage industry whistleblower.
