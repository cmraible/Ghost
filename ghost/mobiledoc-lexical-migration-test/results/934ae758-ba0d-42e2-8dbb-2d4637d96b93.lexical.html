Check out how you can take home Ford's fastest production Mustang yet.

Ford came out swinging when they introduced the all-new 2020 Shelby GT500, the fastest production Mustang to date. Not only did Ford nail its aesthetic with an aggressive yet stunning exterior, it has plenty of power on tap to eat Hellcats for breakfast. Whether you're ready to hit the track or just want to cruise those scenic backroads, this car is a perfect fit no matter the driving situation. If your dream is to get behind the wheel of one of these insanely quick and gorgeous 2020 Shelby GT500, make a donation to support the Shelby American Collection and be automatically entered into the drawing to win one incredible track beast. The winner also receives a GT500 Track Attack experience at Ford's Performance Racing School and a $1,000 gift certificate for Adam’s Polishes.

Finished in a gorgeous Ford Performance Blue topped with painted white stripes, this high-performance Shelby is ready to turn some every head in town. Even better, this one comes equipped with the Carbon Fiber Track Package. A set of 20-inch exposed carbon fiber wheels wrapped with Ford Performance-spec Michelin Pilot Cup Sport 2 rubber (305/30/R20 front, 315/30/R20 rear) sit on all four corners, and a GT4 track wing can be found out back. More exterior highlights include HID headlamps with LED signature lighting, hood vent with a rain tray, rear diffuser. illuminated Shelby puddle lamps, and a larger front splitter and splitter wickers.

Under the hood sits a supercharged 5.2-liter cross plane crank V8 engine backed by a 7-speed dual-clutch transmission that twists a limited-slip Torsen differential with 3.73 axle ratio, and it is equipped with a composite carbon fiber driveshaft. This setup generates 760-horsepower and 625 lb/ft of torque straight out of Ford's factory gates. Handling is provided by an advanced suspension with an independent rear and MagneRide damping system. Stopping is just as important as going so massive Brembo brakes (6-piston calipers up front, 4-piston out back) can be found behind each carbon fiber wheel, the most powerful brakes in a production Mustang. For track use only, the car comes with launch control and Electronic Line Lock. Offering additional rumble and performance is the dual exhaust with active valves and quad tips. Other parts include the Magnesium Tower to Tower Brace, and the coolers for the engine oil, transmission, and differential.

Open the doors to a cockpit full of modern technology. Recaro leather-trimmed sport seats can be found in front of a rear seat delete. Interior highlights include a carbon fiber instrument panel and a Technology Package including a B&O premium sound system that bumps your SiriusXM radio, a 12-inch LCD Digital Instrument Cluster with MyColor(R), SYNC 3 with 8” LCD Touch Screen with voice-activated navigation, selectable-effort Electronic Power-Assist Steering (EPAS), Integrated Driver Control (IDC) system, BLIS (Blind Spot Information System) with Cross-Traffic Alert, Dual-zone Electronic Automatic Temperature Control (DEATC), Intelligent Access with push button start, auxiliary gauges, and an intelligent garage opener. USB ports can be found throughout to charge your phone while pairing it to the car, and a rear view backup camera keeps everything in view when backing out. The car also comes with a tire inflator and sealant kit, and a full vehicle cover (WeatherShield with Shelby GT500 logo).
