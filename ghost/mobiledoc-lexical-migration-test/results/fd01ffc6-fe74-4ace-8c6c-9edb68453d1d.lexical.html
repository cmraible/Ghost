Overview

The 1970 Nova changed little from the previous year with the exception of new side markers, and the taillight lenses were now wider and positioned differently.

The 1970 Nova changed little from the previous year with the exception of new side markers, and the taillight lenses were now wider and positioned differently.

1970 was the final year the 396 could be optioned in. The 350ci/300hp would become the top performance option, with only a handful of 396 Novas being produced in 1970.

Approximately 177 COPO Novas were ordered, with 175 of those converted by Yenko Chevrolet, and the other two were sold in Canada.
