Only the very biggest titles have any realistic chance of playing across the majority of these points in the arc. And that’s where choices come into play - which tools are best for the audience you want to reach? Are they hungry, edge-of-the-seat news consumers? Or are they lean-back, in-depth readers? Are they going to enjoy rich, multimedia features, or so they just want a 60-second explainer video, or a 250 word inverted pyramid job?

Getting that right is the essence of content strategy. Hopefully, you now have enough of an understanding to start making some smart decisions about where you should play - and I strongly recommend using analytics to test and learn from those experiments.

But one thing is certain: the answers online are more diverse than they are in print - and it’s up to you to figure out what your readers want.
