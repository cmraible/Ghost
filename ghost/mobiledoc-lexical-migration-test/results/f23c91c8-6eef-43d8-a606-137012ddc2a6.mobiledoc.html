Abortion Pills Become More Accessible

On Tuesday, the Food and Drug Administration (FDA) finalized a rule expanding the availability of abortion pills to include local drugstores and large pharmacy chains such as CVS Health and Walgreens.

Abortion pills are used in more than half of pregnancy terminations in the country. Until now, mifepristone ‚Äî the first pill in the two-drug abortion medication process ‚Äî could only be offered by a select few mail-order pharmacies. According to the FDA, more than 3.7 million people have used mifepristone since its approval.
