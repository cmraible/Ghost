Be one of the few to own this German masterpiece.

Some cars are instant classics the minute they roll out of the factory This 2020 Mercedes-Benz AMG GT R Pro is just such a creation, combining unbelievable athleticism with killer looks and laudable German craftsmanship. As the ultimate in performance and style, there’s little doubt this car will continue to attract attention as its auction wraps up on Bring A Trailer.

We’re not overstating things when we say this Mercedes hits like a sledgehammer. A twin-turbo 4.0-liter V8 puts 577-horsepower and 516 lb.-ft. of torque at your command. Helping put that power to the road is a rear-mounted 7-speed dual-clutch transaxle. This car comes with the Pro package, which means revised tuning for the transmission, limited-slip differential, and dynamic engine mounts as well as adjustable coilovers for even better performance.

A black on black color scheme, carbon-fiber rear spoiler and roof, large front splitter, and fender vents add to the sinister nature of this coupe. All that black also helps the forged twin-spoke alloy wheels really pop. Wrapped around those are Michelin Pilot Sport Cup 2 tires to help take full advantage of the rear-axle steering feature and AMG High-Performance Ceramic Composite Braking System.

As you’d expect from a German sports car, the interior is both comfortable and functional. Black leather upholstery on seats with tall, supportive side bolsters keep you firmly planted in place. A flat-bottom steering wheel with center line adds to the motorsports-inspired vibe of this car, while the Burmester sound system and Comand infotainment make this the perfect ride for cruising around town or hitting the open road.

The auction for this 2020 Mercedes-Benz AMG GT R Pro ends on Bring A Trailer November 24, so you better act quickly.
