How to download the Betway App?

For both Android and iPhone device users, the Betway app can be downloaded from the official site, but Android users will first have to follow the instructions on how to prepare their phones for the download.

Download on Android

From settings on the Android device, the user can tap on security. They then need to click on “Allow Unknown Sources” in the Device Administration. When the box appears the user needs to click “OK” then go back to the webpage, where they can then click on “Download the Android App”. When the box appears they need to tap “OK” and the app will begin the download. While downloading in the notifications at the bottom of the screen they will be able to see the progress. As soon as the app has been downloaded they can click “Install” and once the installation is complete they can click “Open” and then sign up or log in.

Download on iPhone

IPhone users will be redirected to the iTunes Store once they have clicked onto the Download on App Store icon from the web page. The app will be directly downloaded onto their phone where they will be able to log in with their existing username and password.
