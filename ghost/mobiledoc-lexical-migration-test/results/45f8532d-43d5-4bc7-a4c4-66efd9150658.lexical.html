Overview

The 1967 Corvette is still regarded today as the best selling ‘Vette.

The 1967 Corvette is still regarded today as the best selling ‘Vette. It was restyled with five smaller front fender vents, new rocker panel trim, slotted Rally wheels, and slight interior changes, including a parking brake moved to the center console. This would be the last year of the second-generation Corvettes. The 427s got a different “power bulge” hood and more top horsepower.

The very rare L88 was produced this year featuring a four-barrel Holley carb, and new aluminum heads that raised compression to 12:5:1 and sat on an aluminum intake manifold. GM rated the beast at 450hp, but experts believe that number to be closer to 600 for this Corvette!
