Restoring a vehicle such as this one was quite a large project but the team was dedicated to their dream of making history once again with this beautiful car. Each part has been completely refurbished. Everything from the chassis to the drivetrain has been inspected and revitalized to reinvigorate the spirit of the original Ferrari 375 F1 racer. Special attention was put towards the gearbox as it helps to transfer power from the beautiful 4.5-liter V12 engine and allows for smooth and quick transitions from gear to gear.

When all is said and done this incredible beast will dawn the bright Rosso Corsa red once again to drive the track at the 2021 British Grand Prix at Silverstone Raceway. This restored racer will be driven by Charles Leclerc, a world-renowned successful F1 driver. Finally, this car will feel the heat of the asphalt once again for the first time in over 10 years.
