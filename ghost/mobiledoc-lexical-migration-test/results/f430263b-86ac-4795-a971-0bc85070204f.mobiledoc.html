In 1993, the Viper received air conditioning as a dealer-installed option. City gas mileage dropped to 13 mpg but was picked up on hwy now achieving 21 mpg (previously 17).

The crude street fighter, with little in the daily drivability department (no true top, no outside door handles, and no side windows), saw an increase in production this year up to 1,043. It seems as though a '90s Cobra was a working formula.

More colors undoubtedly helped the increased sales. Instead of just Viper Red, customers had three other options.
