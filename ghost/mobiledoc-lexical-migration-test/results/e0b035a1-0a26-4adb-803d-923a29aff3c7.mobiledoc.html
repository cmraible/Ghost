Trophy #11

This trophy requires you to be in the Volcano, having just escaped from the jail cell. When you go through one of the first vents, you’ll notice that there are two vents above, but the wind current stops you from going into the left one – that’s where our trophy is!

So head through the right vent and zap the robot.

Jump down a level and use the other laser panel to destroy the other robot.

Now jump over to the other side and lift the door. Now that we’ve destroyed the robot, he won’t detect us when we do.

Head inside that vent and deactivate the panel in the top-left corner. As you can see, this will close a door at the top of the other vent, allowing us to get to the left vent.

Step outside the vent where we came in from and activate the laser panel to the left.

Head into the vent that we first went through and now we can ride the wind current up to the top-left vent.

And there’s it is!

Trophy #12

This one is nearly impossible to find if you don’t have the X-tay glasses. So if you didn’t buy them, that’s what this guide is for.

We’re going to want to be in the frame with the six platforms. Go through the vent to the bottom right and turn on the wind fan. Ride the wind up and go through the middle vent. Once outside, jump to the bottom right platform and turn on the vent in there. Now head out, jump down and go through the vent in the bottom right corner again. Make your way all the way to the top but before you continue through the top right vent, the trophy we’re after is in a secret area in the top-left corner. I know, impossible to find if you don’t have the X-ray glasses, but there it is.

Trophy #13

The first one is in the level 1. Head up here, using the grappling rings to hoist yourselves up there.

Have one stand on top of the other and then jump up through the zigzags in the wall.

And there it is!

Trophy #14

The next one is in level 2. When you walk past the three circles on the ground where you’e just opened the door, grab onto the rope to the right which will hoist you up-left.

And right above the platform is what we’re looking for… so grab onto the grappling ring and swing your way up there!

Trophy #16

This one is in level 8. As you keep walking to the right and see the basketball hoop, fall down.

Make your way over to the left a little and the zigzags in the wall reveal a little present for the two of you!

Trophy #17

For the final Trophy, there are a few landmarks you should note. Here, walk across the tightrope/bridge…

Press the buttons at the same time and fall down…

Use the matchstick to boing back up…

Enter through the zigzag in the wall to the left and go boing again on the spring button that’s in the corner…

When you’re airborne and are right at the top, use the grappling rings to make your way over to the left…

And there will be more zigzags in that wall that has the very last one just sitting there waiting for you.
