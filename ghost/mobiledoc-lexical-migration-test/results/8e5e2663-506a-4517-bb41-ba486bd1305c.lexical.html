So far the C8 Corvette has been hailed as the fastest base model, the most beautiful ever made, and the first mid-engined ‘Vette. Unfortunately, it is also the hardest modern ‘Vette to get your hands on. While everyone was hoping for 2021 to be a little better, it is actually not trending that way and as of the end of January, less than 5,000 2021 C8s have been produced. These production delays that have carried over from 2020 have deprived even some of the biggest dealer’s lots of the new Corvette making it harder and harder to buy but who needs to buy one when you can win one?
