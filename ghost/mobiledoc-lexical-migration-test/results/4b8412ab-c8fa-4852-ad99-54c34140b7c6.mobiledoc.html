Rarely seen in the United States, this rare classic Alfa Romeo Montreal can be yours.

Marcello Gandini with the Carrozzeria Bertone introduced the Alfa Romeo Montreal to the public in 1967 at the Expo 67 World’s Fair in Montreal, Canada. The public gave the model a great reception and so the automaker made it a production model. Once it did go into production, only 3,925 were made, and of those, only 100 made it to North America. Examples like this model are extremely rare to find in the U.S.

A German doctor took possession of this car new before importing it to the United States. This example would remain in the country with all three owners, being bought by the current owner in 2014.

Under the current ownership, the car was given a comprehensive cosmetic and mechanical refresh. The color was changed and interior cared for. This car was featured in a 2016 edition of a Playboy magazine photo shoot, which is included with the sale of the car.
