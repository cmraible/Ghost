Overview

The 1952 DeVille styling stayed much the same as the previous years. The year 1952 was also Cadillac's 50-year anniversary and they were calling this model of cars their "Golden Anniversary Cadillacs."

The 1952 DeVille styling stayed much the same as the previous years. The year 1952 was also Cadillac's 50-year anniversary and they were calling this model of cars their "Golden Anniversary Cadillacs."

The big news, however, was a bump in power output. Up from 160 to now 190, Cadillac raised the V-type's compression and used a 4-barrel carb instead of the 2-barrel.

The Hydra-Matic auto also received an update claiming quieter, smoother, and more economical operation with two different forward driving ranges.

Production was up yet again, but by a mere 10%.
