The world has many dimensions that the human eye cannot even fathom. Genesis Noir is a non-traditional point and click adventure where you'll see everything from the Big Bang to the galaxy trillions of years passed. Meanwhile in the present, No Man has caught himself in a love triangle in the middle of a gun shot fired toward his love Miss Mass. See the universe like never before with a 1930s jazzy soundtrack to boot.

Good

  • Clever puzzles
  • Twilight Zone-esque, out of this world, themes.
  • Impeccable art style
  • Contrasting colours that pop
  • That smoooooth jazz soundtrack
