On Wednesday 2/16 at 7:00 pm ET (4:00 pm PT), Daily Poster paying subscribers will chat with Donald Cohen, author of The Privatization of Everything: How the Plunder of Public Goods Transformed America and How We Can Fight Back.

Cohen founded the In the Public Interest, a nonprofit research and policy center dedicated to shared prosperity and the common good. In his new book, co-authored by Allen Mikaelian, Cohen chronicles longtime efforts to turn public goods into private profit centers, and offers up a road map for how citizens can reverse the trend.
