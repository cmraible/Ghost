A few weeks ago, I mentioned in passing that I was working on a new structure for this site. With that comes a set of deadlines that should get me to into a position where I could generate the sort of flow of posts here that I've aspired to for a long time.

Well, it's finally done, and will be launching later today. I'll make a more general post about it a week or two in, once I'm happy it's running the way I want it to. I'll explain the rationale in more detail then. For now, though, here's a preview for email subscribers only of what's coming. (I'm using Ghost's new ability to send emails without publishing a post on the web to do this…)

Here's the planned weekly minimum on the site:

Mondays: Technical Tips

A short explanation of something technical that you might need to know to do better journalism: SEO, social, multimedia and the like. Something defined, useful and actionable. Sometimes they'll be timely, like the one that will go out later today. And sometimes they'll just be useful.

Wednesdays: tl;dr

A short, snappy digest of links to the things you might need to know as a working journalists: developments in search, social, audience strategy and journalism practice.

It'll be broken down clearly into sections, so you can find the ones that are relevant to you. And there will be no more than a sentence or two on each link.

If it's a quiet week, it'll be short. If it's a busy one, it'll be longer. But it'll never be long.

Fridays: Adam's Analysis

(Temporary name while I think of something better. Suggestions desperately appreciated…)

I'll do at least one weekly analysis piece, either on a topical subject, or on some element of journalism or publishing practice that has been on my mind in recent days or weeks.

For the Future

If this works, and I start to see a big enough uptick in subscribers, I'll start adding:

  • Interviews
  • Explainers on relevant subjects

But let's not run before we walk!

Other Posts

It's possible, once I get into the swing of this, that I'll be doing other posts through the week as well. What I haven't decided yet is if I'm going to push everything out as emails.

  • Everything I publish sent as emails
  • A weekly digest of posts I've published outside the structure outlined above?

Hit "reply" and let me know…
