On Thursday 12/9 at 1:30 pm ET (10:30 pm PT), Daily Poster subscribers will speak with the one Democratic congressperson who voted against the $1.85 trillion Build Back Better package: Rep. Jared Golden of Maine. He says he did so because he was opposed to provision that would provide new state and local tax (SALT) deductions to the wealthy, which The Daily Poster has been covering for months.

“Democrats have to make a decision: are we the party of the working class or the party of the millionaire class?” Golden recently wrote about his vote. “We can’t be both.”

Sirota will discuss the cap issue with Rep. Golden, as well as explore why the lawmaker has bucked the party line on other high-profile legislation.

Like all our live chats, the event is exclusively for Daily Poster supporting subscribers, who will be able to watch the broadcast live, ask questions in real time, and watch a replay of the event.
