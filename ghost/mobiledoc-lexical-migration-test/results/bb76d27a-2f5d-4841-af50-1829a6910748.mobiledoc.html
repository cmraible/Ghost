As we previously announced, tonight at 3/24 at 7 pm ET (4 pm PT), The Lever is kicking off its new monthly happy hour series, where supporting subscribers will get to chat with our reporters and editors about anything and everything. David Sirota, The Lever’s founder and editor in chief, will lead the chat from Los Angeles, where he is attending the Academy Awards as an Oscar nominee for Don’t Look Up.

These happy hours will occur every month, allowing our supporting subscribers to interact in real-time with our staff about news of the day and to help us plan our future coverage.

Like all our live chats, these Happy Hours are exclusively for supporting subscribers of The Lever, who get to watch the chat live, interact with our staff, and watch a replay after the event.
