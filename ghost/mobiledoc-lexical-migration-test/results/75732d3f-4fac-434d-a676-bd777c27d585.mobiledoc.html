This wonderful wagon is a rolling piece of artwork, and it is going up for auction.

If you need any help figuring out what the best decade was for automotive design, look no further than this 1957 Oldsmobile Super 88 Fiesta. With its swoopy body lines, this family hauler was one seriously stylish station wagon, and these cars are as rare as they are beautiful.

The big grille and rear fins are icons of '50s designs, and combining that with production of less than 9,000 examples, this 1957 Oldsmobile Super 88 Fiesta is sure to get looks wherever it goes. This Oldsmobile has definitely undergone a certain level of restoration with beautiful paint job matching the car's original two-tone Gold Mist and Victoria White not to mention a stunning amount of flawless chrome and beautiful white wall tires. The listing says that the interior is all new and factory correct, while the vintage-looking aftermarket adds modern conveniences like a CD player and Bluetooth connectivity.

Inside and out, this station wagon looks pristine, but what really makes this Fiesta a party machine is the 350 cubic-inch V8 under the hood topped with a Holley 600 CFM four-barrel carburetor and backed by a 700R4 automatic transmission. These attributes will definitely makes this station wagon a fun family hauler, but the power steering, power brakes and air conditioning mean this car is one cool cruiser. Whether you're looking for a car to show off or cruise in, this 1957 Oldsmobile Super 88 Fiesta station wagon is definitely a unique classic.

This rare and stunning 1957 Oldsmobile Super 88 Fiesta will cross the auction block at Raleigh Classic Car Auctions upcoming Summer 2020 Raleigh Classic, which takes place August 21-22, so be sure you're registered to bid.
