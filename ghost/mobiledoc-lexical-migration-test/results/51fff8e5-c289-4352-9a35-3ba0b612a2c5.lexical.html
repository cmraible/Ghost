Only 1 of 1,550 Silver Arrow limited-edition examples produced for the United States market.

Built as a commemoration to the Silver Arrow Grand Prix racers from the 1930s, Mercedes-Benz created a 2002 Mercedes-Benz SL500 Silver Arrow Edition. Only 1,550 of these were created for the United States market. This stunning Mercedes-Benz Silver Edition SL500 is being offered by Motorgroup LLC.

Each example was painted in the unique shade of Silver Arrow Metallic (777) and equipped with a black and silver leather interior. Protecting the cabin is a contrasting black top. The exterior comes equipped with an AMG body kit that includes a front spoiler, side skirts, and rear air dam. Further complementing the outside is a set of 18-inch two-piece factory wheels on all four corners, and xenon headlamps light up the night.

As mentioned above, the car is equipped with black and silver leather upholstery on the inside, but the cabin also includes electrically-adjusted heated bucket seats, automatic climate control, and a factory stereo. Housed in the dash is a 160-mph speedometer, a tachometer, analog clock, and digital odometer. The mileage on this example reads 56,790.

Powering the car is a 5.0-liter M113 V8 shifted via a 5-speed automatic transmission. This attractive sports car has an output of 302-horsepower and 339 lb/ft of torque. Helping to bring the car to a halt are cross-drilled brake rotors.
