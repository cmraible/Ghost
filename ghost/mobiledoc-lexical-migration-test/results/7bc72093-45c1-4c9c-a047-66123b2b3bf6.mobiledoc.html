Carlisle Auctions is pleased to offer this ultra-rare 1972 Buick GS Stage 1 convertible. A rare gem, only 81 of these cars were produced, and this is just 1 of 2 that sport this color combination. That's right, only two cars in existence with this color combo make old-school Buick Gran Sport a rare car indeed. This numbers-matching classic is set to cross the auction block on the weekend of November 8-9, 2019 starting at 10 a.m. in Sarasota, Florida.

Cloaked in a beautiful Fire Red, this classic Buick is in excellent shape. A vinyl black top protects the roof and adds welcoming contrast to its glossy red paint job. Factory Rallye wheels sit on all four corners and add to its muscle car curb appeal. Inside its tan interior is a replica vintage-style radio that bumps tunes through an amplifier.

Tucked inside the highly detailed engine compartment is a large and built 455cui V8 that generates an impressive 560-horsepower and 581 lb/ft of torque. An automatic transmission shifts all the power down to a 3.73 posi rear end. According to the description, no expense was spared when building the engine and transmission, and everything on this car has been fully documented.
