What you're looking at here is a wonderfully restored 1969 Ford Mustang Mach 1 that is currently up for sale through Art & Speed Classic Car Gallery

The Mach 1 has a distinct design thanks to the chin spoiler, blacked-out hood with scoop and pins, rear window louver, side stripes and the rear decklid spoiler, and this fully restored example looks amazing painted in its original color, Acapulco Blue. The inside of this Mustang is just as clean as the outside, and its use of two-tone accents on the seats and carpet makes it quite the looker!
