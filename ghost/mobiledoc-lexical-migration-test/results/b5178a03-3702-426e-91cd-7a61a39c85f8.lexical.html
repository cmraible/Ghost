Would you add a Mack truck to your car collection?

Not all car collections have to feature high-end luxury cars or performance muscle cars, some of them feature cars that are a little different than the norm. With some of the ‘typical’ players going for insane prices, collectors are looking outside of the classic Bentleys, Mustangs, Camaros, and Cudas to find something to add to their collection. If this sounds like you, here are some unique Mack trucks that are going to be available at the upcoming Tom Mack Auctions event. Here are two such examples to check out ahead of the event.

1958 Mack Model B-61 T

With a Holmes twin boom Wrecker, diesel engine, and single screw, this is a running, driving, and operating truck and wrecker. See it here.

1948 Mack EH

This single screw, gas engine truck has a 10-speed transmission. It was restored and parked in a museum in running condition 10 years ago. It’s now a non-running project that could be your next overhaul. See it here.
