It's that time of year again where we come together to celebrate the best and most notable game releases for the year. Our team has worked together to vote and come up with their favourite games of the year over 11 categories. We wanted this year's game of the year to be an event, therefore we'll be announcing the winners throughout the week. And here we go:

  • Boomerang Fu
  • Hades
  • Manifold Garden
  • Ori and the Will of the Wisps
  • Paper Mario: The Origami King

And the winner is... Paper Mario: The Origami King!
