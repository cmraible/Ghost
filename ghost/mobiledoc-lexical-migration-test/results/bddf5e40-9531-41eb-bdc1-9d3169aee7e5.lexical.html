Hertz Car Rental and Hendrick Motorsports teamed up to create a handful of brand new limited-edition rental Camaros for car enthusiasts to rent, and they could choose between SS and ZL1 trim. Not just your average stock Camaro rental, these cars have been modified inside and out to ensure ultimate thrills behind the wheel after leaving the rental counter. GAA Classic Car Auctions is excited to offer the opportunity to own a Hertz-Hendrick 2020 Chevrolet Camaro SS model that is now open for bids through its online auction.

Clad in a black paint with bright yellow distinctive stripes, this Camaro SS stands out from the crowd. Unique 20-inch Satin Black wheels are bolted to all four corners and come adorned with unique center caps with Hendrick Motorsports branding.

Under the hood sits a naturally aspirated 6.2-liter V8 engine equipped with the Performance Upgrade Package that includes a Chevrolet cold air intake and cat-back dual exhaust system. With a few tweaks, this Camaro SS is capable of 480-horsepower. A strut tower brace with Hendrick Motorsports branding sits inside the engine bay reduces chassis flex.

Behind the doors sits an interior that has also been customized. Embroidered headrests feature the No. 24 team logo along with Byron's signature Hertz fender badges. Even custom Hertz lighted door sill plates are illuminated once the doors are opened. Each Hertz-Hendrick Motorsports Camaro features a unique plaque that denotes its individual number with this example being number 85 out of 200.

If interested in owning this attractive and powerful Hertz-Hendricks 2020 Chevrolet Camaro SS, visit GAA Classic Car Auctions and be sure to register and place your bid. There's no denying that this rare Camaro will turn some heads while cruising down the road. The chances of another one of these in town are likely slim.
