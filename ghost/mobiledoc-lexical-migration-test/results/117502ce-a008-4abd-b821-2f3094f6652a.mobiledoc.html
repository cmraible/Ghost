Make room for one of these bold classic trucks.

The vintage truck and SUV market is absolutely on fire. There’s more demand for these vehicles now than ever before, and demand is outpacing trucks and SUVs currently for sale. In addition, many of these vehicles weren’t well cared for during their time, so finding a good example is even harder to come by. Currently on the docket of the Carlisle Auction's All-Truck Hour are three examples that are collector grade, and ready to enter your collection, or grab one as your daily driver to make everyone jealous of your commute!

1968 GMC 1500

The 1968 GMC trucks sported the classic style, and the future ahead of it. This example is powered by a 402 cubic inch engine, is lowered, exhales through dual exhaust, and rolls on Hoosier tires. See it here.

1986 Dodge 150 Ram SE Royal

This 1986 Dodge 150 Ram SE Royal Truck is a super car pickup. It is loaded up with options and is 100-percent rust-free. It’s an original survivor with no modifications and is a stunning example. See it here.

1998 Jeep Cherokee

If you’re looking for a vintage off-roader, look no further. This 1998 Jeep Cherokee SW has four-wheel-drive, and only 35K original miles. It needs nothing but a new owner. See it here.
