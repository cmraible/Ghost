Overview

The 1950 DeVille changed little with the 1949 model arriving later in that year.

For the 1950 Cadillac DeVille year model, the auto manufacturer changed very little, due to the fact that the 1949 model was arriving later in 1949 year, giving the 1950 little time to breathe on its own.

The public began taking notice of the stylish, and very accommodating DeVille and sales more than doubled from the previous year. With this momentum, the popular Cadillac DeVille was ready to go full-steam ahead in 1950.
